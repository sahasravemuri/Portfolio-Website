Environmental affects on asthma (2023)

This innovative research project explores the intersection of technology and healthcare, combining my interests in medicine and computer science.

Project Overview

For my science fair project, I investigated the impact of environmental triggers on asthma, focusing on how air quality affects respiratory health. I collected and analyzed data from different neighborhoods, comparing asthma prevalence with pollution levels, pollen counts, and humidity. Using this data, I identified patterns and potential correlations between environmental factors and asthma flare-ups. My findings highlighted the importance of clean air in preventing respiratory issues, especially for vulnerable populations.

Impact

I built a website that pulls real-time environmental data and alerts users, especially those with asthma, about days with potentially harmful conditions. My goal was to create a practical tool that helps people manage their health by staying informed. This project emphasized the link between public health and environmental justice, especially in underserved communities with poor air quality. I was honored to receive the DiIorio Foundation Award at the Science and Engineering Fair of Houston (SEFH) for this work in the health and medicine category.
